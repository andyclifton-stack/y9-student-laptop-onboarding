Leaving the School

When a student leaves Claremont, the school will remove management from the laptop and return ownership fully to the family.

What happens when a student leaves?

The laptop will be reset as part of the offboarding process. Students and parents will receive notice to back up any files they want to keep before the reset takes place.

What to back up

Students should save any personal files they want to keep to personal cloud storage, a USB drive, an external hard drive, or another personal device.

After offboarding

Once the process is complete, the device will no longer be managed by the school. Families may then add personal accounts or reinstall personal software if they wish.

Windows edition

The IT team will not downgrade the operating system from Pro or Education to Home.

School files stored in school systems may no longer be accessible after the student leaves, depending on account closure and school data retention processes.
