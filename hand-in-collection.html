Hand-In and Collection

All laptops must be handed in by Friday 3rd July 2026 to guarantee they are ready for September.

Laptop hand-in

Deadline: Friday 3rd July 2026. This deadline gives the IT team enough time to prepare devices before September.

Where should students hand in the laptop?

Students should hand their laptop and charger to their form tutor by Friday 3rd July 2026.

Is an appointment needed?

No appointment is needed. Devices will be processed in the order they are received.

What must be handed in?

  • Laptop.
  • Charger.
  • Stylus or digital pen, if already purchased.
  • Any required pen charger or cable.
  • Laptop bag, if used.

Collection

Families will be advised when the laptop can be collected in person from the school office, or when it will be ready for the student on their return to school in September.

Late hand-in

Late devices will still be processed, but the school may not be able to guarantee they are ready for the start of term. Devices handed in by Friday 3rd July 2026 will be prioritised.
