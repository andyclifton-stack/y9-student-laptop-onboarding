Support and Repairs

For questions about student laptop onboarding, please email office@claremontschool.co.uk.

What support will the IT team provide?

The IT team will support the school-managed software and settings on the laptop. This may include sign-in issues, school Wi-Fi, Microsoft 365 access, school software, Smoothwall or SentinelOne issues, Intune management issues and school configuration problems.

How to request help

Parents should email office@claremontschool.co.uk. When reporting an issue, please include the student name, year group, device make and model, a clear description of the problem, any error message shown, and when the issue started.

Parent responsibility

The laptop remains the responsibility of the student and parents. Parents remain responsible for hardware damage, accidental damage, loss or theft, broken screens, faulty keyboards, faulty chargers, battery deterioration, repair costs and insurance if arranged.

Insurance

Parents may wish to consider insurance for accidental damage, loss or theft. This is optional and should be arranged directly by parents if required.

Lost or damaged stylus

The stylus is part of the student's equipment. If it is lost or damaged, parents will need to purchase a replacement.
