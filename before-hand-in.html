Before You Hand In the Laptop

Please complete these steps before bringing the laptop to school.

Before hand-in. Please complete the registration form and back up important files. The laptop will be reset as part of the setup process, so existing files, apps, user accounts and settings will be removed.

Complete the registration form

Please complete the Year 9 Laptop Registration Form before handing in the laptop. The school will use the details provided to send the required DocuSign agreement and prepare the device for setup.

The Google Form is not the agreement. The agreement will be sent separately after the form is submitted and must be completed before IT can set up the laptop.

Complete Registration Form

Back up anything important

Save files to a personal cloud account, USB drive, external hard drive or another computer. The school cannot take responsibility for personal files left on the device before setup.

Include the charger

The charger must be handed in with the laptop. Please clearly label the charger with the student's name and class.

Label the laptop or bag

Include the student full name, class or tutor group if known, parent contact name and parent contact email. Please do not put decorative stickers on the outside of the laptop.

Write down the password, if there is one

Please do not enter passwords or PINs into the Google Form. If the laptop already has a password or PIN, include it securely with the laptop when it is handed in. Missing or incorrect passwords may delay setup.

Check the laptop powers on

Please check the laptop turns on, the charger works, the screen is not damaged, the keyboard and trackpad work, and the stylus is included if already purchased.
