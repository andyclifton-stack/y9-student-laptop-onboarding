Safeguarding, Filtering and Monitoring

A school-managed laptop allows Claremont to apply appropriate filtering, monitoring and security controls whether the device is used in school or at home.

Why is the laptop managed by the school?

The main reason is safeguarding. School management helps the school meet its safeguarding responsibilities while supporting effective digital learning.

What does Smoothwall Monitor do?

Smoothwall Monitor runs in the background and checks for specific safeguarding risks, such as self-harm, bullying, violence, exploitation, harmful online behaviour and other safeguarding risks.

It does not watch everything a student does. It does not access cameras or microphones. It does not record all normal day-to-day typing. Alerts are only raised when specific safeguarding concerns are detected.

Who sees safeguarding alerts?

Safeguarding alerts are handled by the school's Designated Safeguarding Leaders and authorised members of the safeguarding team. The IT team does not review safeguarding content unless specifically asked to support a safeguarding concern.

Filtering and monitoring remain active at home, at weekends and during holidays while the laptop is school-managed.

Using an unmanaged personal device or hotspot to avoid school systems is not permitted and may be treated as a safeguarding or behaviour concern.
