What IT Will Do

The IT team will prepare each laptop so it is ready for school use.

What happens during setup?

Check

The team will record device details, check the laptop meets the required specification, and check the charger and stylus.

Prepare

The laptop will be reset, Windows may be upgraded if required, and school security settings will be applied.

Install

The school will install safeguarding, security, Wi-Fi and approved learning software.

Why does the laptop need to be reset?

The laptop must start from a clean and secure setup. Resetting removes previous accounts, settings, software and files so the school can configure the device correctly.

Parents and students must back up any personal files before hand-in. The reset is a normal part of the setup process.

Will parents or students have administrator rights?

No. While the laptop is managed by the school, students will use standard school accounts. This means they will not be able to install unapproved software or make system-level changes.

Can parents add another user account?

No. While the device is managed by the school, only school-issued accounts can be used. Personal or non-school accounts cannot be added while the device is under school management.

Will Windows be downgraded when the student leaves?

No. When the student leaves, the school will remove management and reset the device, but the IT team will not downgrade Windows from Pro or Education back to Home.
