Using the Laptop

Students will use their current ClassLink credentials. Their username and password will not change.

Signing in

Students will sign in using their current ClassLink credentials. Their username and password will not change.

School Wi-Fi

The laptop will be configured to connect to the correct school Wi-Fi automatically.

Home Wi-Fi

The laptop can still connect to home Wi-Fi in the usual way. School management does not change the home router or other devices.

Battery and charging

Students should bring their laptop to school fully charged each day. Classroom charging points are limited.

Saving work

Students should save schoolwork in school-approved cloud storage, such as OneDrive.

Software

Only approved software can be installed. If a student needs software for schoolwork, parents should email office@claremontschool.co.uk.

Personalisation: Students may be able to personalise some basic settings, but some settings will be restricted by school management. External stickers are not allowed because school-managed laptops need to remain clearly identifiable.
